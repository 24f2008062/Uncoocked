## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Uncooked Portal**, the premier campus event discovery and management platform! 

Whether you are looking to discover the next big hackathon on campus, or you are an organizer preparing to host a massive cultural festival, Uncooked provides a seamless, all-in-one platform to make it happen. 

### Intended Users
* **Attendees**: Students and professionals looking to discover, register, and securely store tickets for events.
* **Organizers**: Event hosts looking to create events, manage attendees, broadcast announcements, and analyze real-time event performance.

### System Requirements
* **Supported Browsers**: The latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge.
* **Mobile Compatibility**: Fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices (iOS and Android browsers). No dedicated app download is required.

## 2. Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform
Open your preferred web browser and navigate to the official Uncooked Portal URL. 

### Creating an Account
1. Click the **Sign Up** button on the top right of the homepage.
2. Enter your Email Address, Full Name, and create a secure password.
3. Complete the brief **Onboarding** flow by selecting your interests (e.g., Programming, Cultural, Startups) so we can recommend the best events for you.
4. Click **Submit** to finalize your registration.

### Logging In
1. Click the **Log In** button on the top right.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Click **Sign In**. You will be redirected to your personal dashboard.

### Logging Out
1. Click on your profile avatar in the top right corner of the navigation bar.
2. Select **Log Out** from the dropdown menu.

## 3. Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you will be taken to your **Personal Dashboard**, which serves as your central hub.

* **Navigation Bar**: Located at the very top. Use this to quickly jump between the **Home** (Event Explorer), your **Dashboard**, and your **Profile**.
* **Registered Events**: A list of all upcoming events you have successfully booked tickets for.

## 4. Feature Guide

### Hosting an Event (Organizer Role)
**Purpose**: Create a new event and publish it to the public Explorer.
**Step-by-Step Instructions**:
1. From your Dashboard, click **Host New Event**.
2. Fill in the **Event Title**, **Category** (e.g., Hackathon, Fest), and **Date and Time**.
3. *Note: Location is currently locked to Lucknow.* 
4. Upload an **Event Banner** using the built-in image cropper, or select a preset image.
5. Set the **Ticket Type** (Free or Paid). If Paid, set the price in Rupees (₹).
6. Set the **Capacity** (maximum number of attendees) and toggle whether you want to enable a Waitlist.
7. Click **Publish & Host Event**.

### Organizer Command Center
**Purpose**: Manage your live event.
**When to Use**: After publishing an event, click on it from your Dashboard to manage it.
* **Settings**: Edit the event details, change the banner, or update the capacity.
* **Attendees**: View a live list of registered users. Switch tabs to view users on the Waitlist.
* **Analytics**: View interactive graphs showing your event's daily page views and revenue generation.
* **Announcements**: Post live updates to the event's public bulletin board.

## 5. Event Management (Attendee Role)

### Browsing and Searching Events
1. Navigate to the **Home** page to view the Event Explorer.
2. Use the **Search Bar** to type keywords (e.g., "AI Workshop").
3. Use the **Category Filters** on the left sidebar to narrow down events by type (e.g., Meetups, Parties).

### Registering for an Event
1. Click on an event card to view the full **Event Details** page.
2. Click the glowing **Register** button.
3. A registration form will pop up. Your Name, Email, and Phone Number may be pre-filled if you are logged in.
4. *If the event is paid*: Enter your payment details in the secure payment section.
5. Click **Confirm Reservation** (or Pay).

> [!WARNING]
> The application is currently operating exclusively in Lucknow. Ensure you can physically attend the venue before booking paid tickets!

### Downloading Your Ticket
1. After successful registration, a Ticket Modal will appear.
2. Click the **Save as Image** button at the bottom of the ticket.
3. A high-resolution `.png` file containing your unique QR code will be downloaded to your device.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Save this ticket image to your phone's photo gallery! You will need to present the QR code at the venue for scanning and check-in.

## 7. Notifications and Bulletins

Instead of traditional push notifications, Uncooked uses **Live Bulletin Boards** directly on the event pages.

### Viewing Announcements
1. Navigate to an Event Details page.
2. Scroll down to the **Official Bulletin Board** section.
3. Here, you will see real-time updates broadcasted by the event organizer (e.g., "Schedule Changes", "Venue Updates").
4. Organizers can "Pin" critical announcements to the top of the board.

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I lost my ticket image. How do I get it back?**
A: Navigate to your **Dashboard**, find the event under "Registered Events", and click on it. You can view and re-download your ticket from there.

**Q: Why was I put on a Waitlist?**
A: The event has reached its maximum capacity set by the organizer. If an existing attendee cancels, waitlisted users may be moved to the confirmed list.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I log in?**
A: Currently, password resets must be handled manually by the support team. Please contact support using the details at the bottom of this manual.

**Q: My QR code isn't scanning at the venue.**
A: Ensure your screen brightness is turned up and that you have downloaded the high-resolution PNG version of the ticket rather than taking a blurry screenshot.

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Possible Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cannot log in** | Incorrect email or password. | Double-check your spelling and ensure Caps Lock is off. |
| **Ticket won't download** | Browser pop-up blocker is active. | Allow downloads from the portal in your browser settings and click the download button again. |
| **"Currently operating in Lucknow" warning** | User resides outside the designated MVP region. | The platform is currently geo-locked to Lucknow venues. You may still register if you plan to travel. |
| **Event not showing on dashboard** | Registration failed or payment did not process. | Re-visit the event page and attempt registration again. Ensure your internet connection is stable. |

## 10. Best Practices

* **Keep your profile updated**: Ensure your email and phone number are correct so organizers can reach you in case of emergency venue changes.
* **Download tickets early**: Do not rely on venue Wi-Fi. Download your `.png` ticket to your phone's local photo gallery before leaving home.
* **Use modern browsers**: For the best visual experience and smoothest ticket generation, keep your browser updated.
* **Organizers - Be Descriptive**: When hosting an event, provide a highly detailed description and a clear itinerary to reduce attendee confusion.

## 12. Glossary

* **Attendee**: A user who discovers and registers for events.
* **Organizer**: A user who hosts and manages events via the Organizer Command Center.
* **Dashboard**: Your personal home base where you can see your upcoming booked events and events you are hosting.
* **Event Explorer**: The main public page used for searching and filtering through all upcoming campus events.
* **Bulletin Board**: A live messaging feed on the event page where organizers post critical updates.
* **QR Code**: A scannable square barcode found on your digital ticket, used by organizers at the venue to check you in.
* **Waitlist**: A queue you enter when an event is fully booked.
